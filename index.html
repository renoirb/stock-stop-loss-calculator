Stock Stop Loss & Re-entry Calculator

Recommended Levels

Conservative (2% Account Risk)
Moderate (5% Account Risk)
Aggressive (8% Account Risk)

Order Types & Confirmation Strategy:

For Exit: Use a Stop Limit Sell Order
- Set Stop Price at the calculated stop loss level
- Set Limit Price 1-2% below Stop Price to ensure execution

For Re-entry (Trend Reversal Confirmation):
1. Value Entry: Simple Limit Buy at the re-entry price if you want to catch the exact bottom.
2. Momentum Confirmation: Stop Limit Buy with:
- Stop Price: 1.5% above re-entry point
- Limit Price: 1.5% above Stop Price

Why This Works:
- Prevents buying during continued downtrend
- Confirms upward momentum before entry
- Provides a reasonable price range for execution
- Protects against false breakouts with the limit price ceiling
